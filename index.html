YOUR MISSION

The year is 2063. Earth's deep space monitoring stations detected them three weeks ago - a massive alien armada advancing through our solar system. Within days, they began their assault. Colony after colony fell silent. No warnings. No negotiations. Only systematic destruction.

You were stationed on a research outpost when you intercepted a coded transmission. Your blood ran cold as you decrypted the coordinates - Earth was your next target. Your home. Your family. Everything you knew would be reduced to ash within 48 hours. The colonial defense forces were scattered, overwhelmed by the alien swarms.

In a desperate gambit, you infiltrated an alien supply depot and hijacked their prototype fighter - the SOLAR PHANTOM. Its technology is unlike anything human-made: faster engines, devastating firepower, and alien shielding systems. You barely escaped with your life, but now you have a chance.

Time is running out. The invasion fleet is closing in on Earth and the remaining colonies. You must defend every planet, destroy every alien ship, and buy humanity time to evacuate. Scavenge resources from space debris, upgrade your ship, and show these invaders that humanity won't go quietly into the night. The fate of Earth - and everyone you love - rests in your hands.
